About Us

Hyderabad's most trusted cosmetics and salon equipment wholesaler — serving beauty professionals for over a decade.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Beauty

Sri Sai Beauty Centre was founded in the heart of Hyderabad with a single vision — to become the most trusted and affordable cosmetics wholesaler for beauty professionals across the city.

Located at Ram Nagar, one of Hyderabad's busiest commercial areas, our store has grown from a small beauty supply outlet to a comprehensive wholesale hub stocking 50+ top beauty brands including Lakmé, L'Oréal, Maybelline, Biotique, VLCC and many more.

Over the years, we have built lasting relationships with hundreds of beauty parlours, salon owners, makeup artists, and retail cosmetic shops across Hyderabad and Telangana. Our commitment to authentic products, competitive wholesale pricing, and personalized service has earned us a loyal customer base and a strong 4.1 Google rating from 259+ happy customers.
